Introduction

Sex education is a comprehensive education program that addresses human sexuality, including human sexual anatomy, sexual reproduction, sexual activity, reproductive health, emotional relations, reproductive rights and responsibilities, abstinence, birth control, and other aspects of human sexual behavior.[1]

Importance in Society

Comprehensive sex education plays a crucial role in promoting public health and well-being. Research has shown that effective sex education programs can help reduce rates of teenage pregnancy, sexually transmitted infections, and promote healthier relationships.[2]
