Introduction

Self repairing, also known as self-improvement or personal development, refers to activities that improve awareness and identity, develop talents and potential, enhance quality of life and contribute to the realization of dreams and aspirations.[1]

Professional Support

While self-improvement can be pursued independently, professional support can provide valuable guidance and tools. Mental health professionals, life coaches, and counselors can help develop personalized strategies for growth and healing.[2]

Types of Professional Support

  • Psychotherapy
  • Life coaching
  • Career counseling
  • Support groups
