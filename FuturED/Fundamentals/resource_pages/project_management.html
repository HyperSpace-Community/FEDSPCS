Introduction

Project management is the discipline of planning, organizing, and managing resources to achieve specific goals within defined constraints. It involves applying knowledge, skills, tools, and techniques to meet project requirements.[1]

Best Practices

Following established best practices helps ensure project success and minimize risks.[2]

Planning

  • Clear objectives
  • Detailed scope definition
  • Risk identification
  • Stakeholder analysis

Execution

  • Regular monitoring
  • Clear communication
  • Change management
  • Documentation
